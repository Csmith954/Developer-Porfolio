Hi, I’m Chris Smith and I’m a software developer. I completed a coding bootcamp at Tech Elevator where I learned skills in Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Spring Boot, Vue.js, PostgreSQL and Git. I have a background in retail leadership where I lead a team of 30+ across the state of Oklahoma. While in role I developed skills in customer service, team management and sales. During my time in retail, I helped create, test and deploy a new company training program. I also earned 3 “Achievers” awards (the top 10% overall performance) and was featured in the company newsletter for outstanding customer service. In addition to my retail experience, I also have experience as a small business owner. This experience taught me a lot about keeping clients happy, purchasing products, collecting payments, creating marketing materials, determining the value of goods, creating images of products, identifying strategic business opportunities, and developing business plans.

I converted a van into a tiny home which I use to travel the country. These hobbies show that I have a creative side and enjoy spending time in nature. They also show that I am resourceful and have experience with DIY projects.

I’m excited to bring these skills to a new industry and learn more about web development.
